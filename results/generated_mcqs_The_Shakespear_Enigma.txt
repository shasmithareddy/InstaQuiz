## MCQ 1
Question: What was unusual about the pears on the Shakespear tree?
A) They were poisonous.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like apples.
D) They grew upside down.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe the Shakespear tree could do?
A) Grant eternal life
B) Give a glimpse into the future
C) Cure any illness
D) Bring good luck in love
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter
B) To become a writer
C) To become a dancer
D) To become a gardener
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question: What happened after Emma ate the Shakespear pear?
A) She fell asleep for a hundred years.
B) She experienced a whirlwind of visions.
C) She forgot everything she knew.
D) She turned into a pear.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 5
Question: What effect did Emma's actions have on the village of Eldermere?
A) It became deserted.
B) It became a hub of storytelling.
C) It became famous for its pear orchards.
D) It became a tourist destination for its magical tree.
Correct Answer: B
